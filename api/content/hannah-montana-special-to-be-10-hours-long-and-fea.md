In a bold move to recapture the magic of its heyday, Disney has announced that the Hannah Montana 20th Anniversary Special will not only explore the show's impact but also include an exhaustive history of Miley Cyrus's hair styles from seasons 1-4. "We wanted to give the fans something truly special," said a spokesperson for Disney. "And by 'special' we mean a countdown of all 17 different ponytail variations Miley rocked during her time on the show."

The special will also feature an epic interview with Billy Ray Cyrus, who will regale viewers with stories of his own personal struggles, including being forced to listen to Miley's music for 8 hours straight while driving cross-country. "It was a real bonding experience," he said in a statement.

In addition to the never-before-seen content, the Hannah Montana 20th Anniversary Special will include a montage set to the tune of "Best of Both Worlds" that is guaranteed to make you cry, laugh, and possibly question your life choices.

To commemorate this momentous occasion, Disney+ has released an official Hannah Montana Collection that includes all four seasons of the show, as well as Miley Cyrus's hit singles from 2007-2010. And because we know what you're thinking, they've also included a nonstop stream of Billy Ray's music from the same era, because who doesn't love a good "Achy Breaky Heart" repeat?

The Hannah Montana 20th Anniversary Special will premiere on Disney+ on March 24, 2026. Mark your calendars for what promises to be an unforgettable viewing experience that you'll surely regret in 10 years when your kids are asking you to explain the concept of a "vlog."
