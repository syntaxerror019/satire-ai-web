The City of Sandy Springs Recreation and Parks Department has announced its latest attempt to separate you from your hard-earned cash: a weekly Tai Chi session on the City Green. Because what could possibly go wrong with paying $25 for some elderly people in a field to slowly wave their arms?

According to sources, participants will be led by Florin Szondi of the USA Chen Taiji Center, who is undoubtedly an expert in the ancient art of making you pay money to pretend to be a relaxed and harmonious person. Don't worry, it's totally worth it - after all, who needs actual inner peace when you can buy it for $25?

As part of this absurdly-priced package deal, you'll also receive:

* A chance to breathe deeply in the fresh air
* An opportunity to watch Florin Szondi's impressive mustache twirl with each downward dog
* The thrill of possibly getting hit by a stray golf ball from the adjacent course

Don't miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime experience! Join us every Wednesday at 5:00 p.m. and pay $25 to find inner peace - or, you know, just buy a participation trophy and call it a day.
