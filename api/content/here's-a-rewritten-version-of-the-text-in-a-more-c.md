**Long-Term Care Options**

1. **Independent Living**: $3,000-$4,000/month
2. **Adult Day Care**: $2,167/month (avg.)
3. **Assisted Living**: $5,900/month (avg.)
4. **Continuing Care Retirement Communities (CCRCs)**: $3,747/month (avg.) + entrance fee ($300,000)
5. **Adult Family Homes & Medical Foster Care**: $1,500-$3,000/month
6. **Nursing Homes/Skilled Nursing Facilities**: $9,277-10,646/month (avg.)
7. **Memory Care**: $7,899/month (avg.)

**Medicare Coverage**

* Medicare does not cover long-term care costs
* Covers up to 100 days of rehabilitation or convalescent care in a skilled nursing facility

**Paying for Long-Term Senior Care**

1. Personal savings
2. Retirement and pension plans
3. Proceeds from selling real estate or a reverse mortgage
4. Annuities and other investments
5. Long-term care insurance
6. Medicaid
7. Veterans benefits
8. Other government programs and grants

**Financial Planning for Long-Term Care**

1. Learn about available long-term care options in your community.
2. Work with a trusted financial advisor to develop a tailored plan.
3. Explore community resources.
4. Incorporate specific wishes into the plan.
