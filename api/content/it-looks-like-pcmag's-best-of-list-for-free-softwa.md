**Free Software & Services**

1. **Proton VPN**: A top-rated free VPN that offers robust security features.
2. **Avast One Basic**: A reliable free antivirus program with robust protection against malware and ransomware.
3. **PreVeil**: A business-grade email encryption service at no cost.
4. **Proton Pass**: A powerful password manager with unlimited passwords, syncing, and storage.

**Free Audio & Video Editing**

1. **Audacity**: A popular open-source audio editing software for podcasting, music production, and sample editing.
2. **Google Photos**: A free photo storage service with automatic backup, organization, and editing features.

**Free Language Learning & Chatbots**

1. **Duolingo**: A top-rated language learning app with interactive lessons and exercises for over 30 languages.
2. **ChatGPT**: A powerful AI chatbot that offers peerless response quality in terms of accuracy and detail.

**Free Productivity & Organization**

1. **Todoist**: An excellent to-do list app with task management, tracking, and collaboration features.
2. **Tinder**: A popular free dating app for finding potential romantic connections.

**Free Mobile Payments & Website Builders**

1. **Apple Pay**: A convenient mobile payment service that lets you purchase items with your iPhone or Apple Watch.
2. **Wix Website Builder**: An excellent website builder that offers powerful tools and a free plan to get started.

**Free Music & Video Streaming**

1. **YouTube Music**: A music streaming service with ad-supported offerings, community uploads, and recommendation-based listening features.
2. **Tubi**: A video streaming platform that offers major studio releases for free, with some ads and occasional content removal.
